# fundamentals-in-AIML--PROJECT-VITYARTHI
<BR>**OVERVIEW**
<BR><BR>
Proposed project is an AI powered FemTech application which plans to fill the significant research and diagnosis gap concerning female health problems particularly those with scarcity of available resources like endometriosis and PCOS. The application uses a state-of-the-art NLP interface to interact with a machine learning based prediction engine capable of being used across multiple life stages of users. A pubescent user may use the platform to receive accessible and developmentally-appropriate education on menstruation; an adult woman may use it to record chronicity of chronic symptoms, medications, and hormonal fluctuations during perimenopause. The agent is acting as a digital triage for users by taking into account users-reported symptoms and user-provided medical histories and identifying the possibility of presence of possibly hidden diseases and degree of severity for existing ones.
<BR>
There is great emphasis on user safety and the system contains a logic layer to flag any clinical "red flags" and recommend to user to ask opinions from health care professionals. By concentrating on female-specific health data, the model attempts to validate and quantify the type of pain and suffering in conditions such as endometriosis, that are often overlooked and dismissed by healthcare professionals. Beyond being a predictive tool for women and their healthcare providers, the model functions as an informational repository and data collection point, that will arm women with detailed and predictive information about their health. Essentially, this project attempts to provide access to a highly-specialized domain of healthcare and use it as both a means of early detection, and a reliable, flexible source of well-being from adolescence through menopause.

In the terminal, type: "python-m streamlit run app.py"
